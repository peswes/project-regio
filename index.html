Coperative Goals

Vision

The vision is to become a leading edible oil manufacturer operating with a smart and cost-effective business model.

Mission

The mission of the company is to operate a production plant committed to excellence and quality

Medium/Long term goals

Once the proposed 2MT/HR palm kernel extraction in installed and in full operation at a steady state, we have the plans in place to acquire a 20MT/DAY semi continuous edible oil refining plant.

Why Choose Us?

We bring solutions to make life easier for our clients.

  • Markets for the major products from the proposed extraction plant is in high demand in the country. Bearing in mind that at a point, most of the bigger refiners depended on importing crude palm kernel oil and palm oil to meet their factory running capacity until the recent ban by the government on the importation of vegetable oil to support local production, hence the a

  • From the beginning of setting up of the current plant, the project sponsor has developed a vast experience in the edible oil extraction industry. With the proposed new plant, additional experts in various aspect of the business will be hired to meet up with the scale-up operation. The combined experience spanning over 20 years in the manufacturing industry gives the ability to identify individual(s) with the relevant skills to handle all aspects of the business

  • The 2MT/HR palm kernel extraction plant will be sourced from Asia, China to be precise. (Henan Doing Mechanical Equipment Co Ltd). Based on our industry experience and research, we resolve to go with the Chinese technology because of its efficiency, high oil yield, low energy consumption and cost effectiveness compared to others equipment manufacturers in the market.
