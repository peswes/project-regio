History

The Company (Regio Manufacturing & Logistics Ltd) was incorporated in 2007 to manage and optimize processing of oil palm plantation produce (Palm oil, Palm kernel oil & Palm kernel Cake) from a 179 Hectares plantation established in 1995. The main objective of this document is to demonstrate that proposed project is a viable and profitable venture

The proposed project will be focused on palm kernel fruit processing to derive palm kernel oil (PKO) and palm kernel cake (PKC) as its main products. Palm kernel oil is one of the main raw materials used in the manufacturing of edible vegetable oil while palm kernel cake is used for animal feed compounding.

Currently, the Company has installed capacity for processing up to 20MT/DAY of palm kernel per day. The existing plant was acquired fairly used from Sudit Oil Ibadan Oyo State while taking over operations and assets of Best Oil Ibadan. Due to wear and tear, the extraction rate of the currently mill as significantly dropped, making the current operation not cost effective. The Company is hereby looking to procure a fully integrated 20MT/DAY palm kernel processing mill with a 46% extraction rate. Considering the nature and the peculiar business terrain of the country, we are basing our projections for this project on a conservative annual production estimate of 2649.6.4MT of palm kernel oil, 2800MT of palm kernel cake.

Conservative sales revenue will be generated in the first year of operation and will grow steadily at a yearly average of 10% subsequently. The projections contained herein are authentic and will be used as the budget for the business.

In addition, palm kernel shells (PKS) which is considered as a waste material hence no real economical value in Nigeria because it is used as substitute for firewood has value in some foreign markets
