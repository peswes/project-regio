Manager

The Managing Director/CEO of the Company, Prince Adebambo Olugbemigun, has a BSc in Pharmaceutical Science and HNC Chemistry from the University of Greenwich. On graduating, he worked in the Pharmaceutical, Cosmetics and Nutrition industry in the United Kingdom at various capacities (Research & Development, Quality Control / Assurance, Manufacturing, Project Management, Regulatory and Compliance).

From the beginning of setting up of the current plant, the company has developed a vast experience in the edible oil extraction industry. However, with the proposed new plant, additional experts in various aspect of the business will be hired to meet up with the scale-up operation. The combined experience spanning over 18 years in the manufacturing industry gives the ability to identify individual(s) with the relevant skills to handle different aspects of the business.

The promoter of the business Prince Adebambo Olugbemigun will oversee the management and operation of the business with the following core management staff

Project Manager

This position will be occupied by a competent manager with the relevant skill sets to manage raw material procurements as well keep an eye on procurements to ensure that cost and projections are adhere to and possibly reduced

Plant Technical Manager

The role will be filled with a technically competent manager who will oversee day to day running of the plant, plan production schedule with project manager and the entire production team, manage training of all plant staff and ensure that maintenance of all equipments are performed as at when due.
